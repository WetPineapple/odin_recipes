Meatloaf

This is Meatloaf! The recipe and the band

This meatloaf dates back ages ago. Back to when a time was young and the loaves had to yet to awaken. Back then the elders tell of an individual who shall reunite the meat. This is that tale

Ingredients

  • Groud beef or Rock
  • onions or drums
  • ketchup or bass

Steps

  1. preheat over to 325
  2. loaf the meat and morph it into a ball
  3. ball that loaf up with onions
  4. bake in over for 38minutes
  5. remove from oven and slather with ketchup
  6. searve and eat
